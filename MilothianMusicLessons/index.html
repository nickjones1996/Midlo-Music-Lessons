About Our Lessons

We are professional musicians who have established a studio to focus on teaching music lessons. We don't work out of a music store, so we don't get commisions on instrument or equipment sales and we are free from having to meet high student census quotas. This means that our teachers are focused entirely on making sure their students get the best musical instruction possible and that any recommendations they make are for the good of the students, not the gain of a commision.
Every teacher on our roster is college or conservatory educated in music and have a clean background. In addition to being highly trained in their field, most of our teachers are also well-known performers. Since many of our teachers are also performers, they understand the value in playing in front of an audience and offer oportunities for their students to play in recitals.
