Guitar Lessons with Jack, Tony and Tim

Jack Jones

Jack Jones is a professional guitarist who has taught in the Richmond area since 1986. Jack has taught hundreds of students how to play guitar. Many of Jack's students are active in the central Virginia area at both the amateur and professional level, and some of his former students are actively pursuing teaching careers of their own today.

About

  • Founder of Midlothian Music Lessons
  • Over 30 years of experience teaching
  • Graduated from VCU Magna Cum Laude in 1991 with a degree in music
  • Offers Beginner, intermediate and advanced lessons starting at age 5
  • Offers Lessons in popular styles such as rock, metal, reggae, ska, alternative, ect as well as more formal styles such as classical and electric.

    • Jack's approach to teaching:

    "As a teacher my greatest strength is an ability to adapt a traditional sight-reading/theory/ ear training approach to any style of playing. This creates a well rounded and trained player with permanent skills, regardless of whether they study classical, jazz or popular styles." Visit Jack's page by clicking here

    Tony Ruiz

    About Tony

    Tony Ruiz is bi-lingual in English and Spanish, which can help to facilitate communications with Spanish speaking parents that don't speak English or limited English. He gives lessons from beginner to advanced students on guitar and bass as well as beginning piano and Latin percussion lessons.

    Visit Tony's page here

    Tony's approach to teaching:
    Focus on div technique and skills

    Tony caters to each student's unique needs while focusing on making sure they know the fundamentals of music giving them the tools necessary to be good muscians such as:

    • tuning
    • articulating good rhythm
    • tone
    • sight reading
    • solfege(ear training)
